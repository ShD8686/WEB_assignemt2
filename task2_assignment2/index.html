Steve Jobs

Co-founder of Apple Inc.

1955 – 2011

Steve Jobs was an American entrepreneur, inventor, and industrial designer. He was the co-founder of Apple Inc. and is recognized as a pioneer of the personal computer revolution.

Timeline

  1. 1976 – Co-founded Apple Inc.
  2. 1984 – Introduced the first Macintosh computer
  3. 1986 – Bought Pixar Animation Studios
  4. 1997 – Returned to Apple as CEO
  5. 2007 – Launched the iPhone
  6. 2011 – Passed away, leaving a huge legacy

Key Contributions

  • Personal computers
  • Graphical user interfaces
  • Smartphones (iPhone)
  • Animation industry (Pixar)
